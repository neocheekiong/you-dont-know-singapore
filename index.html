You Don't Know Singapore!

Singapore is a small city so we like to think we know it very well.

There is plenty to see and explore, even on an online map.

This game will introduce you to some of the oddball things that you can find on the map.

You will begin to see how to explore the city of Singapore even from the comfort of your home.

Hopefully you will want to go out to visit this places like I did!
